Mark’s Library of Good Things¶

This is my Programmer’s Notebook. I’ve collected various bits of wisdom on my blog, in Google docs, Stack Overflow, local fileage, etc. It’s pretty random, but it will mostly be about computers and programming. Since I generally take lots of notes when I’m learning something, you can consider it an ongoing journal of my ignorance!

This incarnation is a sphinx document hosted on Github Pages. Is that the right way to do things? Got a better way? I’d like to hear about it.

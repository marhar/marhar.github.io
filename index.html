Some hosted web apps:

  • Geographic Center Calculator
    click or search points on a map, see the geographic center. be sure and try the globe view!
  • Historic Stock Returns Calculator (S&P 500)
    backtest your investment strategy against historic S&P 500 returns. Try 1967 for a terrible run and 2010 for a great run.
  • Mortgage Payoff vs Invest Calculator
    reddit would have nothing to discuss if only they would use this calculator and understand that
    (a) it depends, and (b) the missing parameter is risk.

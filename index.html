Welcome to Mark Harrison's github.io!

Some hosted web apps:

  • PID Controller Demo
    Interactive real-time PID tuning and visualization. Part 3 of the East Bay Guide to PID Loops series.

  • Double Pendulum Simulator
    Chaotic dynamics visualization with adjustable parameters, phase space plots, and energy graphs.

  • Geographic Center Calculator
    Click or search points on a map, see the geographic center. Try the globe view!

  • Historic Stock Returns Calculator (S&P 500)
    Backtest your investment strategy against historic data. Try 1967 for a terrible run and 2010 for a great run.

  • Mortgage Payoff vs Invest Calculator
    Payoff or Invest? Try 1967 and 2010 for two different answers.
